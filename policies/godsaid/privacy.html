Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 1, 2024

Introduction

GodSaid ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our spiritual companion application.

By using GodSaid, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile picture (optional)
  • Authentication Data: OAuth tokens from Google Sign-In (we do not store passwords)
  • Spiritual Profile: Religious preferences, meditation goals, and spiritual interests you choose to share

Usage Information

  • Conversation History: Your interactions with the AI spiritual guide
  • Meditation Sessions: Duration, frequency, and preferences
  • Daily Wisdom: Topics you engage with and bookmark
  • App Usage: Features accessed, time spent, and interaction patterns

Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and crash reports
  • Network information (Wi-Fi/cellular)
  • Time zone and language settings

How We Use Your Information

To Provide Personalized Spiritual Guidance

We use your spiritual profile and conversation history to:

  • Tailor AI responses to your beliefs and values
  • Recommend relevant wisdom and meditation practices
  • Track your spiritual journey progress

To Improve Our Service

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Train our AI to provide better spiritual guidance
  • Fix bugs and improve app performance

To Communicate With You

  • Send daily wisdom notifications (if enabled)
  • Provide customer support
  • Announce new features or important updates

Data Storage and Security

Where We Store Your Data

Your data is securely stored using:

  • Supabase Cloud: For account and profile information
  • Local Device Storage: For offline access to bookmarks and preferences
  • Encrypted Servers: For conversation history and AI training data

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive conversations
  • Industry-standard TLS/SSL for data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure authentication via OAuth 2.0
  • Automatic session timeout for inactive users

AI and Machine Learning

GodSaid uses artificial intelligence to provide spiritual guidance. Here's how we handle AI-related data:

  • Conversation Processing: Your messages are processed by AI to generate responses but are not linked to your identity when used for model improvement
  • Anonymization: Training data is anonymized and aggregated
  • No Profiling: We do not create psychological profiles or make automated decisions about you
  • Human Review: No human reviews your personal conversations unless you explicitly report an issue

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your spiritual conversations with anyone
  • Use your data for targeted advertising

We May Share Data:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share
  • For Legal Compliance: When required by law or court order
  • To Protect Rights: To prevent fraud or protect safety
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help operate our service (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights and Controls

You Can:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of all information we have about you
  • Update Information: Edit your profile and preferences anytime
  • Delete Conversations: Remove individual messages or entire conversation history
  • Export Data: Download your data in a portable format
  • Delete Account: Permanently remove your account and all associated data
  • Opt-Out: Disable notifications and data collection features

How to Exercise Your Rights:

You can manage most privacy settings directly in the app under Settings > Privacy. For other requests, contact us at support@eleflytech.com

Children's Privacy

GodSaid is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

If you use GodSaid from outside China, please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in China where our servers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top
  • Sending you an in-app notification for significant changes

Your continued use of GodSaid after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
